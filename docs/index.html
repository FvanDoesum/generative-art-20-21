Generative Art

A creative coding course @CMDAmsterdam exploring generative art with p5.js

Topics

During the 6 weeks of this course we'll cover the following essential processing topics.

Week 1: Basics of generative art and P5.js

We'll cover the basics of what generative art is, introduce p5.js and draw our first Sketch.

Week 2: Randomization and Algorithms

We'll look at how to introduce ranomness inside your sketch to create make your artwork variable.

Week 3: Input and interaction

We'll look at different types of interaction, user input, gestures and multi-device support..

Week 4: Recess

No lessons this week. Go outside and watch video's of cute animals.

Week 5/6: Artwork

No new theory is introduced. You can continue working on your artwork. We'll use lessons for feedback.

Assignment

Create a generative artwork using processing (p5.js) with the overarching theme of this minor; identity (the new intimacy). The artwork is unique and represents you as an individual.

Grading

You'll show the artwork you've created based on the live version and code in your repository. You'll be graded on 4 general topics: creativity, complexity, interactivity & generativity.
